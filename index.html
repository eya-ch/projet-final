About Us

Small Paws is a non-profit organization and plateform that aims to provide a home to abondoned pets all over Tunisia. With Small Paws you can help provide safe refuge, medical care, and new beginnings to these small paws . From innovative medical and behavior programs to investments in outreach and advocacy, we’re advancing animal welfare and creating a more humane world for animals everywhere.

Mission

To engage the hearts, hands, and minds of the community to help animals.

Vision

To compassionately and responsibly create a more humane world for animals.

Core Values

Be good to animals. Partner with people. Lead responsibly with compassion.
